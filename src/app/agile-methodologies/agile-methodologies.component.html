Agile Methodologies

Have led teams using several different Agile Methodologies.

  • Scrum. The most well known of the Agile methodologies, it is also the most widely used. With fixed iterations, it works well for planning and estimating longer term projects.
  • Kanban. For teams that have irregular release cycles or mainly support production, Kanban is an ideal solution.
  • SAFE. The Scaled Agile Framework is a where an organization aligns its development teams to synchronize deliverables.
  • Agile Software. I have experience using Rally and VersionOne.

I have experience developing and leading teams with all three of these Agile methods. I was also an active member of the Agile Council at Kelley Blue Book. This was a team of volunteers to answer that supported the use of Agile best practices throughout Kelley Blue Book. Finally, I have attended two Agile Conferences which was a great experience and I learned a lot about Agile and related topics.
