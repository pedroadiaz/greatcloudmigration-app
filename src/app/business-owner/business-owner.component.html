Entrepeneur

  • Play VR Today. I owned and operated my own virtual reality arcade located in Santa Ana. Although it was highly reviewed and popular, I was unable to turn a profit and it closed its doors for good on July, 2017.
  • RealEstateVR.biz. This was a company and product I created on my own to create 360 virtual tours for real estate agents.

I am a strong believer in entrepeneurship and have followed through with my ideas until the end. Although I haven't been monetarily successful with my idea for a virtual reality arcade, I view it as a success because I brought smiles to peoples faces, people loved the product and the customer service. This is evidenced by the twenty 5-star reviews we had on Yelp. I also view it as a learning experience as I always try to learn from my mistakes and now know some things I would do differently if I had another opportunity.
